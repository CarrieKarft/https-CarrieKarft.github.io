What We Do

The Tabby Abbey strives to provide care, comfort and compassion to animals in need while engaging the hearts, hands and minds of our community to bring about an end to the killing of abandoned and orphaned shelter animals.

Our Adoptable Cats

To addopt one of our furry friends give us a call at: (503) 222-3333

Danny, 6 years, Male
Danny is a fun-loving goofball of a cat. He enjoys playing and snuggling. He will steal all the good seats in your home, and loves to sit and watch TV with his humans. He is good with other pets and childern, and is litterbox trained.

Lewis and Cheryl, both 11 years old, Male and Female
Lewis and Cheryl are littermates. They are bonded and can only be adopted together. Lewis likes his alone time, but is always somewhere nearby. Cheryl is more of a social butterfly who likes to have long conversations with whoever is around. Both cats are litter trained, and good with other pets and children.

Mr. Henry, 13, Male
Mr. Henry is our oldest kitten at the moment. This guy has seen it all, and is looking for a quite home to spend his final years in. He is a loyal companion who will make his way over to your lap everytime you sit down. He prefers a human companion who is home often. Mr. Henry is litter trained, and would prefer a quite house with older adults.

Toot, 10 years old, Female
Toot is 10 years old, but has the energy of a young kitten. She can be a little shy at first, but once she gets to know you she'll be your best friend forever. Her two great loves in this life are peanut butter, and string toys. Toot is litterbox trained, and loves dogs. Children and other cats make her nervous.
